This is the instructions for Teach Teenagers Mathematics at Home project.

1. Power on the Landtiger board
2. Connect the PC and the board with UART 2
3. Connect the BlueTooth module with board in pins 3*3, GND, p0.11 and p0.10
4. Open the terminal on laptop and use correct serial line to have access to board
5. Press the RESET button to get a new virsion
6. Wait to see the second page about questions
7. Press you answer in terminal for each question in a limited time (before you answer, you have long enough time to think about it)
8. Once you finish for all the questions, you will see the score page
9. On terminal, there will show one encouragement sentence
